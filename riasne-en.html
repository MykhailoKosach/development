Riasne

The complex is located on a 1.79 hectare plot and combines modern technical solutions with well-planned infrastructure.

  • 7,235.6 m²
  • Type: dry, refrigerated
  • Class: B+

Technical Specifications

  • Working height of warehouses – from 8.8 m to 10 m
  • Rack storage of goods – up to 8.8 m
  • 4 sectional gates with dock levelers
  • Own gas boiler house with capacity of 2,376 kW
  • Power supply – 550 kW
  • Artesian well
  • Water supply and sewerage – city systems
  • Energy-saving lighting system
  • Air conditioning of office premises
  • Internal fire water supply and smoke removal system
  • Specialized room for charging stackers

Security and Convenience

  • 24/7 security with video surveillance and access control system
  • Isolated premises for independence from other tenants
  • Availability of zero ramp
  • Spacious parking for 120 vehicles (50 – cars, 70 – trucks)
  • Convenient access roads

An extremely important advantage of the complex is its location at the intersection of two main roads of international communication Lviv-Shehyni and Lviv-Krakovets and directly on the Lviv Ring Road, which is part of the M-06 Kyiv-Chop highway.

After the completion of the planned construction of the Northern Bypass of Lviv, it will become part of the transnational GO Highway corridor, which connects Ukrainian Black Sea ports with Polish Baltic Sea ports in Gdansk and Gdynia.
