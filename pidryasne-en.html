Pidryasne

Located in an industrial zone with developed transport infrastructure and convenient access roads for passenger and large-sized transport.

  • 41,000 m²
  • Type: dry, production
  • Class: A

Technical Specifications

  • Dry warehouse area – 35,000 m² (temperature range from +15 to +24 °C)
  • Office space area – 5,000 m²
  • Utility rooms area – up to 1,000 m²
  • Own solid fuel boiler house – 1.6 MW
  • Power supply – 1,500 kW
  • Backup power supply – 2 diesel generators of 250 kW each
  • Water supply – city systems (133 m³/day)
  • Sewerage – pumping station
  • Energy-saving electric lighting
  • Equipped with ventilation system
  • Equipped with fire safety systems
  • Equipped with compressed air system
  • 4 sectional gates with dock levelers (possibility of installation up to 12)

Security and Infrastructure

  • 24/7 security with video surveillance
  • Access control system
  • Convenient access roads
  • Parking area for passenger and large-sized transport

Facility Advantages

Pidryasne warehouse complex is a modern Class A facility retrofitted for production needs. The combination of large areas, backup power systems and specialized equipment creates efficient conditions for storage, logistics and production.
