Zymna Voda

Located at the intersection of two main roads of international communication Lviv-Shehyni and Lviv-Krakovets and directly on the Lviv Ring Road, which is part of the M-06 Kyiv-Chop highway.

  • 116,000 m²
  • Type: dry, refrigerated
  • Class: A+

Technical Specifications

  • Dry warehouse area: 42,000 m² (temperature range from +15 to +24 °C)
  • Refrigerated warehouse area: 21,000 m² (temperature range -2…+10 °C; -18…-22 °C)
  • Office and utility rooms area: 10,000 m²
  • Working height of warehouse: 12.5 m
  • Rack storage of goods up to 12 m height
  • Column spacing: 12 × 24 m
  • Power supply: 2nd category, 5.5 MW
  • 2 diesel generators for backup power supply of 250 kVA each
  • Solid fuel boiler house 3.8 MW
  • Water supply: 400 m³/day

An extremely important advantage of the complex is its location at the intersection of main roads of international communication and on the Lviv Ring Road. After the completion of the construction of the Northern Bypass of Lviv, the complex will become part of the transnational GO Highway corridor, which connects Ukrainian Black Sea ports with Polish Baltic Sea ports.
